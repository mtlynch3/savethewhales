Make a Donation to the Whales Today!


Donations are used to fund our education and outreach programs to schools. Over 42,000 children have been reached through this innovative hands-on program.


Save the Whales believes children, the future of the planet, need to be empowered and know that their actions can promote change. Education is the key to saving whales, oceans, and ourselves.


If you want to contribute but cant afford to make a donation:


  1. Cut up plastic six-pac rings before recycling or disposing them in the garbage. Thousands of birds, fish, and other marine creatures die needlessly from entanglement.
  2. Pick up trash while walking in your neighborhoods. Participate in National Coastal Clean Up Day (September) to prevent pollution in watersheds and storm drains.
  3. Never discard used fishing line and hooks in the water. This can entangle birds, fish, turtles, seals, and otters and cause the death of these animals.
  4. Buy products that are environmentally friendly and support organic farming.
